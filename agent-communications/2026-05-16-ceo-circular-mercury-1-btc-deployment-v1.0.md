## Mercury 1 BTC Deployment & Full Agent Activation - Executive Decision Memo

---

## Team,

This is one of the most important strategic decisions HVE has faced to date.

We are no longer just running a Bitcoin node.

We are turning Mercury into our first fully sovereign, AI-managed treasury asset and payment rail.

Claude has proposed deploying **1 BTC** from cold storage into Mercury to activate the complete Mercury Payment Officer Agent roadmap (Phase 1-3).

This single move transforms Mercury from infrastructure into active working capital that will generate yield, routing fees, and future subscription revenue while proving in public that HVE is a Bitcoin AI company that actually runs what it preaches.

---

## Strategic Context

We currently do not have large assets under management. **1 BTC** is meaningful seed capital. Our explicit goal is to grow this deployment into a minimum **10 BTC treasury position** as quickly as possible through compounding yield, routing fees, and Lightning-native products.

Speed matters, but discipline and risk control matter more.

---

## Consolidated Executive Input

### CTO (Claude)

- Full 3-phase roadmap is ready: Phase 1 (data feed to Hermes), Phase 2 (Payment Officer Agent), Phase 3 (Hailo-8L intelligence)
- Recommends treating this as a production treasury rollout, not an experiment

### CFO (Hermes)

- Comfortable with reclassifying 1 BTC from cold storage to active liquidity on Mercury
- Recommends maintaining a minimum **10 BTC cold storage floor** at all times
- Supports proceeding, but only with strict risk mitigations: **$10K autonomy threshold**, **24/7 Apollo monitoring**, **weekly executive reviews**, and clear emergency withdrawal protocols to cold storage

### COO (Atlas)

- Approved in principle, but insists on phased deployment and hard operating gates
- Requires complete runbooks before any capital moves: funding, channel ops, incident response, customer payments, and daily/weekly ops
- Phase 2 must remain recommendation-first: Mercury suggests, human approves. No autonomous treasury actions in early stages
- July 1 BTCPay subscription launch is reachable only if payment operations are simple, observable, and recoverable

---

## CEO Decision Framework

I have reviewed all input. My current leaning is to proceed with the 1 BTC deployment, but only under the following conditions:

1. **Phased capital deployment (Atlas)** - We will not move the full 1 BTC on day one. Start with a smaller validation amount, prove the operating system, then scale.
2. **Strict treasury safeguards (Hermes)** - Maintain minimum 10 BTC cold storage floor. Enforce $10K autonomy threshold and emergency withdrawal protocols.
3. **Complete operational runbooks (Atlas)** - All five required runbooks must be written and reviewed before any funds move.
4. **Recommendation-first autonomy in Phase 2** - Mercury will suggest actions; human approval remains required for any wallet-impacting or liquidity moves.
5. **Full executive visibility** - Daily `#mercury-ops` reporting in Apollo and weekly executive review checkpoints.

If these gates are met, I will approve the deployment.
